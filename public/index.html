A dignity-first way to give — donors scan, participants don’t need phones or bank accounts.

Scan a QR code to donate in seconds. Designed so people receiving support don’t need cash, a phone, or a bank account.

DigniPay is a city-backed, shelter-partnered donation system that lets anyone support unhoused neighbors instantly. It keeps choice and transparency in the community while removing friction for donors.

How it works

Shelters onboard participants, DigniPay generates a QR badge, and donors give on any phone in seconds.

1. Register

Partner shelter creates a wallet with minimal details.

2. Give

Donor scans the badge and sends support instantly.

3. Empower

Funds are earmarked for priorities with full transparency.

1. Donor scans QR

Quick scan on any phone to open the donation page.

2. Donates securely

Card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay in seconds.

3. Support without barriers

Participants receive help without phones, cash, or accounts.

Why Montreal chooses DigniPay

Zero-fuss giving

QR or NFC badges unlock immediate giving from any phone or card.

Shelter-led support

Partners help participants set priorities and keep dignity front and center.

Transparent impact

Live dashboards show funding flow and optional donor tips.

Local-first privacy

No apps or accounts for participants, just secure wallets managed by shelters.
